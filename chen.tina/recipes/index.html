MY HEALTHY RECIPES

Chocolate Shake

Ingredient

  • Chocolate Soy Milk
  • Banana
  • Yogurt

Instructions

1.Chop one banana, grab a scoop of yogurt, pour out a cup of choocolate soy milk and place them to a plate.
2.Place all ingredients and some ice into the blender.
3.Blend it for 30 seconds.
Healthy yummy chocolate shake

Breakfast Potato

Ingredient

  • Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Seasoning Blend
  • Garlic Salt

Instructions

1.Chop potatoes to small pieces.
2.Place chopped potatoes to a oven plate, put some olive oil,seasoning blend and garlic salt.
3.Place the potatoes to oven, 450 F for 30 minutes.
Sweet and soft breakfast potatoes

Vegetable Roll

Ingredient

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Spicy sauce

Instructions

1.Fried an egg and melt a slice of cheese with olive oil.
2.Place two pieces of lettuce to a bowl and then put a fried egg,cheese and three slices of tomato,spicy sauce.
3.Wrap the lettuce with all the food inside.
Low calorie vegetable roll
